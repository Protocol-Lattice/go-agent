Why Go‑ADK

Designed for maintainers who care about clarity, performance, and control.

  • Deterministic control over prompts, memory, and tool usage.
  • Composable: swap models, stores, or transports without rewrites.
  • Production‑ready patterns: testing, benchmarking, and CI‑friendly.
  • Interoperability via UTCP for portable tool actions.
